Anu Zochrim exists to preserve the memory of those murdered on October 7, 2023, and those who fell defending the Jewish people and the State of Israel.

Your support helps ensure that each name is recorded with care, dignity, and historical accuracy, and that this memorial remains accessible for generations to come.

Contributions directly support site maintenance, archival research, and the long-term preservation of this public memorial.
