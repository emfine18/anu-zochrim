WE REMEMBER

Anu Zochrim is a public memorial dedicated to the Israelis murdered by Hamas on October 7, 2023, and to those who fell defending the Jewish people and the State of Israel.

This initiative preserves their memory with dignity and purpose, providing a meaningful way to dedicate learning, prayer, and moments of joy in their honor. In remembering them, we affirm the resilience of the Jewish people and our enduring commitment to the security and future of the State of Israel.
