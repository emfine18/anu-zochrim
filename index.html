We Remember

Anu Zochrim is a public memorial initiative dedicated to honoring the memory of Israelis and Jews murdered on October 7 and in the war that followed.

The project exists to ensure that names are preserved with dignity, accuracy, and permanence — and that learning, prayer, and moments of life can be intentionally dedicated in their memory.
