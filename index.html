Anu Zochrim

We Remember

A public memorial documenting those murdered, fallen, and kidnapped on October 7, 2023, and in the war that followed.

This database includes information about civilians, soldiers, police officers, and foreign nationals who were killed or abducted on October 7, 2023, and in the subsequent war.

It records names, ages, places of residence, dates of death, and — where known — the location and nature of the incident. In cases where death occurred at a later date or in a different location, this is also documented.

The information was voluntarily collected and verified using open sources, including media investigations, official memorial sites, and direct communication with families who wished to share or correct information.

Sources & Methodology

The database is based on open and publicly available sources. Names were cross-checked where possible against official memorial sites (IDF, National Insurance Institute, Israel Police), media reports, and additional documentation.

Authority records from the National Library of Israel are linked when available, providing standardized name references and archival context.

This project is independent and non-governmental. Its purpose is documentation, commemoration, and historical accuracy.

View the Database

The database allows searching and filtering by name, residence, role, date, and location.
