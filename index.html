We Remember

Anu Zochrim is a public memorial initiative dedicated to honoring the memory of Israelis and Jews murdered on October 7 and in the war that followed.

The project exists to preserve names with dignity, accuracy, and permanence, and to enable learning, prayer, and moments of life to be intentionally dedicated in their memory.
