About Anu Zochrim

Anu Zochrim (“We Remember”) is a public memorial initiative dedicated to honoring the Israelis murdered by Hamas on October 7, 2023, and those who fell defending the Jewish people and the State of Israel.

The initiative exists to preserve names, dates, and memory with dignity and permanence. Remembrance is not passive — it is an act of responsibility, continuity, and moral clarity.

Anu Zochrim provides a meaningful way to dedicate learning, prayer, and moments of joy in their honor. In remembering them, we affirm the resilience of the Jewish people and our enduring commitment to the security and future of the State of Israel.
