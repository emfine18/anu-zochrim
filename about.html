About Anu Zochrim

Anu Zochrim (“We Remember”) is a public memorial initiative dedicated to honoring the memory of Israelis murdered by Hamas on October 7, 2023, and those who fell defending the Jewish people and the State of Israel.

This project exists to preserve each name with dignity, accuracy, and permanence, and to provide a meaningful way to dedicate learning, prayer, and moments of life in their memory.

Jewish memory is an active commitment. Through remembrance, we affirm the resilience of the Jewish people and our enduring responsibility to the security and future of the State of Israel.
