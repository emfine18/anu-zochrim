About Anu Zochrim

Anu Zochrim is a public memorial initiative dedicated to honoring the memory of Israelis murdered by Hamas on October 7, 2023, and those who fell defending the Jewish people and the State of Israel.

The project was created to serve as a central and accessible space for remembrance — ensuring that each name is preserved with accuracy, dignity, and respect.

Jewish memory is active and enduring. Through learning, prayer, and moments of joy, lives lost are honored with meaning. By enabling searches by name, date, or place, Anu Zochrim allows moments of life to be intentionally dedicated in memory of those who were killed.

Anu Zochrim is non-political and non-commercial in purpose. Its sole mission is remembrance.
