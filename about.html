About Anu Zochrim

Anu Zochrim (“We Remember”) is a public memorial dedicated to the Israelis murdered by Hamas on October 7, 2023, and to those who fell defending the Jewish people and the State of Israel in the war that followed.

This initiative was created to ensure that every name is preserved with dignity, accuracy, and permanence. At a time when memory itself is contested, Anu Zochrim stands as a clear and unwavering record of loss, sacrifice, and moral clarity.

Beyond remembrance, the project provides a meaningful framework through which learning, prayer, and moments of joy can be intentionally dedicated in their memory. In doing so, we affirm the resilience of the Jewish people, the righteousness of Jewish self-defense, and our enduring commitment to the security and future of the State of Israel.
