About Anu Zochrim

Anu Zochrim (“We Remember”) is a public memorial initiative dedicated to honoring the memory of Israelis murdered by Hamas on October 7, 2023, and those who fell defending the Jewish people and the State of Israel in the war that followed.

The project serves as a central, accessible space for remembrance — ensuring that names are preserved with accuracy, dignity, and respect.

Jewish memory is active. Through learning, prayer, and meaningful action, Anu Zochrim enables moments of life to be intentionally dedicated in memory of those who were killed.

Anu Zochrim is non-commercial and non-political in purpose. Its sole mission is remembrance.
