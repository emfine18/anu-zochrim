About

Anu Zochrim (“We Remember”) is a public memorial dedicated to the Israelis murdered by Hamas on October 7, 2023, and to those who fell defending the Jewish people and the State of Israel.

This initiative exists to preserve each name with dignity and accuracy, and to provide a meaningful way for individuals and communities to honor the fallen through learning, prayer, and acts of joy.

In remembering them, we affirm the resilience of the Jewish people, the righteousness of Jewish self-defense, and our enduring commitment to the security and future of the State of Israel.

Founder

Ethan Fine founded Anu Zochrim in December 2025 with the goal of ensuring that we never forget those murdered by Hamas and those who fell protecting the Jewish People and the State of Israel. This initiative is designed to serve as a resource for Jewish communities, families, and individuals across the world to dedicate their Simchas and Learning to ensure that we never forget the names and stories of those whose lives were stolen by evil.

Ethan graduated from Indiana University in May 2025 and currently works in Hedge Fund Credit at a large financial institution.
