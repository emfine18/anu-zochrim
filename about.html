About Anu Zochrim

Anu Zochrim is a public memorial initiative dedicated to honoring the memory of Israelis and Jews who were murdered on October 7 and in the war that followed.

The project was created to serve as a central, accessible resource for remembrance — ensuring that names are preserved with accuracy, dignity, and respect.

Jewish memory is not passive. It is expressed through learning, prayer, and acts of meaning. By allowing users to search by date, name, or place, Anu Zochrim enables moments of life to be intentionally dedicated in the memory of those who were killed.

Anu Zochrim is non-political and non-commercial in purpose. Its sole mission is remembrance.
