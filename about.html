About Anu Zochrim

Anu Zochrim (“We Remember”) is a public memorial initiative created in response to the atrocities of October 7, 2023, and the war that followed. It exists to honor the Israelis murdered by Hamas and those who fell defending the Jewish people and the State of Israel.

The initiative was founded on the belief that remembrance is not passive. Preserving names, dates, and stories is an act of responsibility — one that strengthens collective memory and affirms the moral clarity of Jewish self-defense.

Anu Zochrim provides a structured way for individuals and communities to dedicate learning, prayer, and moments of joy in memory of those who were lost. Through remembrance, we reinforce resilience, unity, and commitment to the future of the State of Israel.

This project is independent, non-partisan, and rooted in the conviction that memory is essential to justice, continuity, and national strength.
